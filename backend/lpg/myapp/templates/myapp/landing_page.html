Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register?
Click on login. In the login page you can find register option. By entering your details and clicking on register, youwill be registered.
How do I book an LPG gas cylinder?
To book an LPG gas cylinder, simply log in to your account on our website, go to the booking in services. After filling out details like preferred slot, date click on book. Your order will be booked.
How can I cancel my order?
To cancel your order, log in to your account, go to the 'Cancel' in services, active order will be displayed. Click on the cancel button to cancel the order before its delivery.
How can I view my past orders?
To view your past orders, log in to your account and navigate to the 'My Orders' in services. There you will find a list of all your previous orders along with their statuses - booked/ out-for-delivery/ delivered.
